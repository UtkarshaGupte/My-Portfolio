Smart Camera Surveillance System

The goal of the project was to achieve an energy efficient smart camera with object detection capability and to achieve video sensing with less storage.

Learnings Involved:

Brainwave Detection

The goal was to detect various spots to identify signals responsible for various emotions in humans and study various brain activities. Major learning of the project was building circuits to amplify, remove noise and filter requisite brain signals for further processing in MATLAB.

Heart Rate monitoring system

The project included measuring the heart rate through the fingertip which was fed to micro-controller interfaced with GSM for wirelessly communicating the message in case of patient's critical state.The main idea behind the project was to notify doctor in case of emergencies.

Learnings Involved:

  • End-to-end designing,
  • programming of GSM module with Arduino

Smart Street Light System

The project was aimed at interfacing of Arduino board with LDR sensors and LED. The goal was to save energy consumption of highway lights.

Learnings Involved:

  • Understanding of different sensors and their integration with Arduino with basic programming
